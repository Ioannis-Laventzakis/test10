Ancient Crete Minoan Civilization

The Minoans are named after King Minos, a legendary figure from Greek mythology. The Minoans were known for their advanced society, which included impressive architecture, sophisticated art, and a complex social structure.

Palace Centers

The Minoans built elaborate palace complexes as their administrative, religious, and economic centers. The most famous of these is the Palace of Knossos, located near modern-day Heraklion.
